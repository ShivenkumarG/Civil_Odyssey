About Us

Welcome to Probable Maximum Precipitation (PMP), where we are dedicated to advancing knowledge and research in the field of hydrology and meteorology. Our team of experts is committed to providing valuable insights into Probable Maximum Precipitation and its applications in various engineering and environmental contexts.

Our Mission

Our mission is to contribute to the understanding of probable maximum precipitation, conduct cutting-edge research, and promote the responsible use of this knowledge in engineering, meteorology, and related fields.
